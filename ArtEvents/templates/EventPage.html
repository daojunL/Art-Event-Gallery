The 2020 World Tour of Justin Bieber

Description

This is going to be a Livestream event of multiple artists in a music festival format. Completely interactive with chatrooms, contests, raffles, sweepstakes, and Zoom video conferencing so friends can create their own watch party on the site. As we know friend groups go stage to stage together at festivals and we want to give that same personal experience. At the end, there will be an awards show for best-performed artists that will be judged by likes during the stream and winners of contests, raffles, and the sweepstakes. This will be streamed on SAFEMusicFestival.com and multiple social platforms all at once!
